The Journey of Web Protocols

Exploring the evolution from HTTP/0.9 to the modern, secure web.

Project Overview

This site documents the transition of the Hypertext Transfer Protocol (HTTP). What began as a simple way to share text files has evolved into a complex system focusing on speed, security, and efficiency[cite: 28].

Key Terms at a Glance

ConceptDefinition
MultiplexingSending multiple data streams over one connection in HTTP/2.
HTTPSThe secure, encrypted version of HTTP.
QUICThe foundation for HTTP/3, optimizing speed.
LatencyThe delay before a transfer of data begins following an instruction.
